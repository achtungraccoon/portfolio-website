About

Hi again! My Name is Katharina Schuldheisz,

I’m a visual designer based in Germany, where I live with my fiancé. I hold a BFA in Graphic Design and a BA in Live & Performing Arts Management. My journey from the music industry to design has been driven by a lifelong passion for design, creativity, and a commitment to crafting clean, impactful, and user-centered designs. I believe that great design is about solving problems and making complex ideas simple and engaging. My approach combines thorough research with a focus on clarity and detail, ensuring every project is clean, functional, and visually appealing. When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me at a concert, traveling, attempting to learn Swedish, or dreaming up my next spooky adventure. I’m open to remote opportunities within the EU and would love to connect and explore how we can bring your ideas to life!
